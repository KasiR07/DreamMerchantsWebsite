About us

DREAM MERCHANTS is the premier business management and finance club of vit. Our objective is to develop an interest among individuals in the field of finance, business, and entrepreneurship. We aim to provide opportunities to students to explore a career in finance and trade while understanding the relation ship between business, economic & monetary disciplines. It is a not-for-profit-organization that aims to develop its member's business insights through various workshops, events, and seminars which are attended by curious young business enthusiasts. We provide a platform for our members to discuss trading and investing along with top industry leaders.

About VIT

Vellore Institute of Technology, Vellore, established in 1984, is one of the top private universities in India. The institution strives for excellence in research, quality of education and student welfare. In August 2019, the Indian government declared VIT as an Institute of Eminence. With students from over 60 countries as well as from every state in India, VIT ensures every individual benefits from education, grounded in ethics and critical thinking, for improvement of life.
